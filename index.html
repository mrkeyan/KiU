Modern Japanese Dining
Experience Authentic Japanese In Markham

Opening in January 2016!

“Kiu” (joyous rain) signifies a renewal of life. Here at Kiu, our goal is to deliver dynamic experiences that will create lasting memories.

Our kitchen will offer a variety of dishes stemming from authentic "Washoku" Japanese cuisine. Along with an assortment of appetizers, salads, and mains that will be served year-round—Ramen Noodles and Yakitori BBQ being but a few of these items to look forward to—a selection of seasonal dishes will also be available.

The Sushi Bar will fuse traditional classics and contemporary trends from Japan together to feature popular styles such as Sashimi, Robatayaki, and more.

Lastly, our Izakaya Bar will serve a broad collection of wine, beer, Sake, as well as a unique list of mixed drinks and cocktails.

Come join us at Kiu’s new location in the heart of Downtown Markham January 2016!
